Pasta Carbonara

Description

This was my favourite dish as a child. The eggy and bacon-y flavour are amazing.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Basil
  • Bacon
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolk You will only need the yolk.
  3. Mix the yolk with some heavy cream
  4. Add salt, pepper and basil to this mix
  5. Fry the bacon
  6. Drain the noodles
  7. Add the sauce and the bacon to the noodles, mix well
