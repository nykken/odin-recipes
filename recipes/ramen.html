Instant Ramen with Veggies

Description

Craving ramen, but want to still feel like you are being healthy? Fear not! This is your solution.

Mix assorted veggies in your ramen so you can still get your 5-a-day.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite instant ramen
  • Leek
  • Garlic
  • Spring onions
  • Ginger
  • Assorted veggies of your choice

Steps

  1. Chop your veggies
  2. Mince the ginger and garlic
  3. Add oil to a pot, sautee the leeks and spring onions
  4. Add the ginger and garlic
  5. Add the rest of the veggies and the ramen flavour powder
  6. Add boiling water, boil the veggies
  7. Add the noodles, wait a couple of minutes
